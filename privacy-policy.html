🔒 Privacy Policy

How we protect your data and respect your privacy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

At LASU GradeTracker, your privacy matters. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe. We are committed to protecting your personal information.

2. What We Collect

When you sign up, we collect:

  • Name (first and last)
  • Username
  • Email address
  • Department and level
  • Password (encrypted — we cannot see it)

We also collect anonymous usage data to improve the platform, such as which tools are most popular.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your profile and badge
  • Enable community features (posts, comments, study buddies)
  • Send important notifications (replies, updates)
  • Improve platform features based on usage

We do not sell your data to third parties. Ever.

4. Anonymous Features

Features like Confessions and Anonymous Drop Box do not require your identity. Even if you are logged in, these posts are stored without linking to your profile. We respect your right to privacy.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

We use local storage to remember your preferences and keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising. You can clear your browser data at any time.

6. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase for authentication and data storage. Google and Microsoft sign-in options are available. These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including encrypted passwords and secure database connections. However, no system is 100% secure. Please use a strong password and keep it private.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise these rights, contact us through the Contact Ochanya page.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the platform evolves. Significant changes will be announced. Continued use means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

Questions about privacy? Reach out through Contact Ochanya or email ochanyaocheme@gmail.com.
