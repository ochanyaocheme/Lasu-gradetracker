Last updated: July 2026

1. Welcome to LASU GradeTracker

By using LASU GradeTracker, you agree to these Terms of Service. This platform is built by students, for students — to calculate grades, connect with peers, and build a supportive community.

If you do not agree with any part of these terms, please do not use the platform.

2. Who Can Use This Platform

LASU GradeTracker is exclusively for students of Lagos State University (LASU). By signing up, you confirm that:

  • You are currently enrolled at LASU
  • You will provide accurate information during registration
  • You are at least 16 years old
3. Your Account

You are responsible for keeping your login details secure. Do not share your password with anyone. If you suspect unauthorized access, contact us immediately.

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate these terms or engage in harmful behavior.

4. Community Guidelines

LASU GradeTracker is a safe space. You agree NOT to:

  • Spam, scam, or promote unrelated content
  • Impersonate other students or staff

Features like Confessions and Anonymous Drop Box allow you to share without revealing your identity. However, anonymity is not a shield for abuse. We reserve the right to investigate and take action on anonymous reports that violate our guidelines.

You own the content you post. By posting, you give LASU GradeTracker permission to display it on the platform. You are responsible for what you share — ensure it does not violate copyright, privacy, or any law.

7. Grade Calculations

Our calculators follow LASU's official grading policy. However, final grades are determined by the university. Always verify critical academic decisions with your department.

8. Termination

We may suspend or terminate your account if you violate these terms. You may also delete your account at any time by contacting us.

We may update these terms as the platform grows. We will notify users of significant changes. Continued use after updates means you accept the new terms.
